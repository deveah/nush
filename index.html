About

nush is a traditional, coffeebreak roguelike set in a lightweight science-fiction universe. You play a treasure hunter who has landed on a former human colony, home to an important research base. Your purpose is to delve the complex and find the Core of Nushangthmpfz, a powerful artifact which is said to be an infinite energy source.

State of the game

The game is in development, so any contributions are highly appreciated. Be sure to send in any bugs you may encounter, and also any suggestions you may have!
