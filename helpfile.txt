Welcome to Nush!

You can scroll this and other screens up and down using the `arrow' keys,
`j' and `k', `pageup' and `pagedown', or `home' and `end'.

   {{YELLOW}} Controls

You can move in 8 directions using vi keys or the numpad (plus arrow keys):
 {{WHITE}} 7 8 9     y k u  {{pop}}
 {{WHITE}} 4 5 6     h . l  {{pop}}
 {{WHITE}} 1 2 3     b j n  {{pop}}
`5' or `.' waits for a turn, or targets self if targetting.
Please turn off Numlock to use the numpad.

Move onto an enemy to perform a melee attack, or onto a closed door
to open it.

       `f' - Fire weapon
  `<' or `>' - Ascend/descend stairs
  `g' or `,' - Pick up items
       `c' - Close door
       `i' - Inventory
`Q' or `Esc' - Quit
       `p' - Show message log
 `F1' or `?' - This screen
 `R' or `^R' - Redraw the screen

Debug keys:
       `$' - Show map
       `(' - Teleport to next map
       `)' - Teleport to previous map
      `F2' - Dump globals
      `F3' - Show curses test screen

   {{YELLOW}} Map icons

     {{yellow}}#{{pop}} - Wall
     {{BLUE}}~{{pop}} - Shallow water
     {{WHITE}}+{{pop}} - Closed door
     {{WHITE}}/{{pop}} - Open door
